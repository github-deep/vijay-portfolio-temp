One day, Lord Shiva faced a perplexing problem.

He went to Lord Brahma to seek a solution. Lord Brahma, the Creator, surely must know since he is the originator.

'Tell me, I'll answer anything', he instructed Shiva.

After Shiva posed his doubt, now there were two Gods in a room breaking their minds, with no solution in sight.

Both approached Lord Vishnu, who joined via a video call.

When Shiva posed his doubt, Lord Vishnu smiled in his quintessential style. But his smile hid his inability to answer!

Now there were three Gods searching for an answer.

Lord Vishnu proposed to ask Lady Saraswati. Initially hesitant, they all agreed.

The three Lords went to her office.
Lady Saraswati greeted them with some coffee.

Pleasantries exchanged. Shiva came to the point and posed the question.

'Does God exist?'

She laughed at first, and answered –

'Areey! It's so simple. Check it out on YouTube. All answers are on YouTube!'

('Areey YouTube pe dekko na, sab milega wahan').
