A writer had a kick-ass script.

He approached an A-list actor. Both met and the writer handed over the script.
The actor promised to revert after a week.

On the 8th day, the writer called the actor.

'Sir, did you read the script?'

The actor had completely forgotten about the script, let alone read it. Busy driving, he promised to get back to the writer shortly.

Pulling his car to the side, he immediately uploaded the script to an AI platform, and asked for '10 reasons I should do the film, and 10 reasons I shouldn't.'

In addition, the actor asked AI to identify one scene each that is weak and strong.

The AI platform gave a summarized response in 10 seconds. The actor took another 20 more seconds to process it in his head.

He called back the writer as promised and rejected the film.

All without reading the script ever.

***

Welcome to the new world!
